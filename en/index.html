Why KoinBuddy

Not another expense tracker. A goal-first way to manage finances.

KoinBuddy starts with what matters to you, then brings statements, documents, insights, and intelligence into one organized space.

Privacy first

Your financial data stays on your device by default, so reviewing money does not need to feel exposed.

Goals without pressure

Plan around what matters at your own pace: an emergency fund, a first home, debt freedom, retirement, or your own goal.

Useful AI assistance

Local AI helps explain spending patterns in plain language without turning your finances into a crowded dashboard.

Documents without clutter

Import bank statements, invoices, and receipts so records can settle into place without manual busywork.

Steady progress

Follow savings, family budgets, vacations, or any goal with a steady rhythm instead of a rush.

Cashflow before it is urgent

See income, spending, recurring costs, and what is left for the future before things feel urgent.
